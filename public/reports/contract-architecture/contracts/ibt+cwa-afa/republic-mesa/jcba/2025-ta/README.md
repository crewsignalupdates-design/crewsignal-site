# Republic / Mesa · Tentative JCBA (Mid-Term) — Shared Representation

This folder preserves the unions’ Joint Tentative Agreement summary for the combined Republic/Mesa Flight Attendant group.

## Representation structure
- Republic Flight Attendants: represented by International Brotherhood of Teamsters (IBT) Local 135
- Mesa Flight Attendants: represented by Association of Flight Attendants–CWA (AFA-CWA)

The unions coordinated a mid-term Joint Collective Bargaining Agreement (JCBA) shortly after merger close.
The Republic contract served as the base framework, with transition protections for Mesa Flight Attendants.

## Scope and purpose
This is a mid-term JCBA framework intended to deliver near-term economic and quality-of-life improvements
while preserving the parties’ path to full Railway Labor Act Section 6 negotiations beginning in January 2027.
