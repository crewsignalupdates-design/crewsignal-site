# Spirit / AFA late-2025 agreement source bundle

This bundle contains the core PDFs I located for the late-2025 Spirit Airlines / CWA-AFA flight attendant agreement and the bankruptcy materials that appear most relevant to it.

## Included files

- `Spirit_AFA_LOA_No_2.pdf`
  - The 11/24/2025 Letter of Agreement between Spirit Airlines and the AFA flight attendants.
  - Extends the amendable date to January 1, 2028.
  - Sets temporary concessions effective January 1, 2026 and date-certain snapbacks in 2028-2029.

- `Spirit_AFA_Bankruptcy_Conditions_Ballot_Certified_Results.pdf`
  - Certified union ballot-results PDF for the bankruptcy conditions vote.

- `Spirit_2025_Q3_10Q.pdf`
  - Spirit’s Q3 2025 10-Q.
  - Confirms Nov. 6, 2025 agreements in principle with ALPA and AFA.
  - Gives bankruptcy context including September 2025 flight-attendant furlough announcement and aircraft/Section 1110 motions.

- `Spirit_2025_Annual_Report_10K.pdf`
  - Spirit’s 2025 annual report / 10-K.
  - States the union deals were ratified in December 2025, approved by the Bankruptcy Court on Dec. 29, 2025, and amendable in January 2028.
  - Summarizes flight-attendant concessions (reduced incentive overtime; elimination of ground holding pay in certain circumstances).

- `Spirit_Bankruptcy_Doc603_Certificate_of_Service_Notice_of_Presentment.pdf`
  - Epiq docket PDF for Doc 603.
  - Publicly identifies the Dec. 11, 2025 “Notice of Presentment” for motion docketed as ECF No. 585.
  - Motion title: authorization to enter agreements establishing new labor conditions with ALPA and AFA.

- `Spirit_Bankruptcy_Doc668_Certificate_of_Service_Doc642-644.pdf`
  - Epiq docket PDF for Doc 668.
  - Publicly identifies the title used for ECF No. 642 in a later service filing.
  - This filing labels ECF 642 as an “Order Pursuant to 11 U.S.C. § 1113 Authorizing Debtors to Reject Collective Bargaining Agreement.”

- `Spirit_Bankruptcy_Doc910_OMM_Second_Interim_Fee_Application.pdf`
  - Epiq docket PDF for Doc 910.
  - Publicly states that ECF No. 585 was the motion to authorize entry into agreements establishing new labor conditions with ALPA and AFA, and that it was granted on Dec. 29, 2025 as ECF No. 642.

## Key web-only materials I relied on

These were available as public web pages in the browser session, but not downloaded here as files:

- Spirit AFA page titled **“Bankruptcy Forced Changes to the Contract, as Proposed”** (Nov. 17, 2025)
  - Shows the earlier proposal with profitability-based snapbacks.
  - Warns that labor contracts had to be approved by mid-December to avoid a court filing to force changes.

- Spirit AFA page titled **“Date Certain Snapbacks”** (Nov. 24, 2025)
  - Explains that member feedback led to a change from performance-based snapbacks to date-certain snapbacks.

- Spirit AFA page titled **“Bankruptcy Conditions Ballot Results”** (Dec. 11, 2025)
  - States the ballot passed with 74% voting FOR.

- Spirit press release dated **Nov. 7, 2025**
  - Announces agreements in principle with ALPA and AFA.
  - States the savings were necessary for the next DIP-financing draw.

- SEC Exhibit 10.1 / DIP Credit Agreement dated **Oct. 14, 2025**
  - Sets financing milestones requiring either labor agreements in principle or section 1113 proceedings for later DIP draws, and amended agreements or rejection for the fourth draw.

## Important note on the court papers

I found a small wording inconsistency in later public documents:

- The annual report and Doc 910 say the court approved entry into the new labor conditions agreements on Dec. 29, 2025 (ECF 642).
- Doc 668 labels ECF 642 as an order under section 1113 authorizing rejection of a collective bargaining agreement.

So the existence/date/docket number are well supported, but the exact title/caption of ECF 642 is described differently in different later public records.
