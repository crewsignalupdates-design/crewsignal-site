Agreement Metadata

  • Baseline analysis: Spirit Contract Analysis

Summary

LOA No. 2 amended the 2023 CBA as a bankruptcy-era concession package, extended the agreement for an additional two-year term, and established date-certain restoration points in 2028 and 2029.
