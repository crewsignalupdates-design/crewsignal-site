First Contract Process

Under the Railway Labor Act framework, a first contract generally proceeds through: (1) bargaining and exchange of proposals, (2) mediated negotiations if impasse persists, (3) tentative agreement, and (4) membership ratification. This page will be updated when a ratified agreement, extension, or controlling side letter is published.
