Background & Framework

This page translates the formal National Mediation Board (NMB) docket into a more readable, aviation-focused summary. Rather than linking to a standalone PDF, Crew Signal presents the core docket items here in HTML for easier search, linking, and regular updates.

Entries are organized chronologically and focus on filings, decisions, and key procedural steps under the Railway Labor Act (RLA) that affect airline labor negotiations, representation disputes, and mediation timelines.
