United / CWA-AFA Mediation

United Airlines and the Association of Flight Attendants–CWA (CWA-AFA) conducted a scheduled block of federal mediation sessions in Chicago during the week of January 6–9, 2026, as publicly communicated in union negotiations updates.

What is confirmed

  • A mediation session block was scheduled and conducted for January 6–9, 2026 (Chicago).
  • The union publicly identified a priorities agenda including: sit rig; targeted scheduling improvements (including electronic notification and redeye flying); improved RAPs; tightened hotel language; and contract enforcement.
  • As of the report date, no public tentative agreement or formal “release” from mediation was announced.

Analytical significance

The occurrence of a multi-day mediation block indicates continued engagement under the Railway Labor Act (RLA) process and a continuing “work-the-process” posture. The absence of public proposal detail is normal mid-cycle and should not be read as either progress or stagnation without additional indicators (cadence changes, clustered sessions, mediated “package” movement, or formal procedural shifts).
