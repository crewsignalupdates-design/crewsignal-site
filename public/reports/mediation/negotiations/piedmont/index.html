Strike Vote Context

During the prior Piedmont bargaining cycle, non-union reporting said Flight Attendants voted unanimously in 2021 to authorize a strike. CrewSignal treats that as a pressure signal, not as a legal countdown to job action.

Under the Railway Labor Act, a strike vote does not by itself authorize a strike. Lawful self-help can occur only after bargaining and mediation are exhausted, the National Mediation Board releases the parties following a proffer of arbitration, and the statutory cooling-off process has run.
