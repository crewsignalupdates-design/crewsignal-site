How to Read These Signals

  • Best progress indicators: recurring bargaining cadence, scope narrowing, and a documented TA pathway.
  • Weak indicators: generic “negotiations ongoing” language with no dates, scope, or milestones.
  • Key inflection points: mediation entry, TA announcement, ratification results, implementation timelines.
