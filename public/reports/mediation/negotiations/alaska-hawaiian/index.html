Joint Collective Bargaining Agreement (JCBA) tracking context for two CWA-AFA-represented groups.

Union: CWA-AFA · Track: JCBA (Alaska + Hawaiian) · Amendable: 2028.02.28 · Updated: 2026.02.20

Status

CrewSignal treats Alaska/Hawaiian as a JCBA pathway: each group’s current agreement remains the governing contract until an integrated agreement is negotiated and ratified.

  • Negotiation type: JCBA (integration pathway)
  • Amendable alignment: Publicly posted contract materials show both groups aligned to an amendable date of 2028.02.28
  • What to watch: formation of integrated bargaining structures, published JCBA milestones, and ratification timelines
