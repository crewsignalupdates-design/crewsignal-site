Negotiations & Mediation Hub

Tracking airline labor negotiations and docketed National Mediation Board cases under the Railway Labor Act, with carrier-level weekly updates.

Editor’s Note: This hub is based solely on publicly available information, including statements from labor organizations, employers, and relevant mediation agencies, as of the report date. It is prepared for informational purposes for crew and labor-focused readers and should not be interpreted as legal advice, bargaining guidance, or a prediction of negotiation outcomes. CrewSignal distinguishes carefully between mediated negotiations, informal talks, tentative agreements, and post-agreement implementation phases when evaluating labor dispute developments.
