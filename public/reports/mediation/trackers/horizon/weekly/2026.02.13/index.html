Status Summary

The strongest public bargaining signal this week came from outside the union. In its 2025 Form 10-K, filed February 12, Alaska Air Group said Horizon flight attendants remained amendable from April 30, 2024, that negotiations for an updated agreement were ongoing, and that a National Mediation Board mediator was participating in the talks.

For lineholders, that matters because it is a company-filed confirmation that the case remained active and unresolved in mid-February. The filing did not point to a tentative agreement, ratification calendar, or release from mediation. It simply confirmed that bargaining with CWA-AFA was still open, still amendable, and still under mediated oversight.

  • Non-union anchor: The clearest public confirmation this week came from Alaska Air Group rather than from union messaging.
  • Case status: Horizon flight attendants remained amendable and still in active negotiations.
  • Process read: No tentative agreement or self-help timeline was disclosed in the filing.
