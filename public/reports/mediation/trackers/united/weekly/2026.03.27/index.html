United / CWA-AFA

NMB Docket: A-14065 · Mediator: Michael Kelliher

Week Ending 2026.03.27

Status Summary

The week ending 2026.03.27 produced the clearest public bargaining breakthrough in this entire reporting cycle. On 2026.03.26, United and CWA-AFA announced a new tentative agreement, shifting the file from late-stage mediation into an internal union review and ratification-track posture.

Same-week public reporting said the tentative agreement included immediate raises upon ratification, top pay reaching $100 per hour by the end of the agreement, boarding pay, new compensation for long gaps between flights, a signing bonus for every Flight Attendant, and a total signing-bonus pool worth $740 million. During the report week, however, the deal remained tentative rather than ratified.

  • Same-week milestone: United and CWA-AFA reached a new tentative agreement on 2026.03.26.
  • Process shift: The case moved from open mediated bargaining to tentative-agreement review.
  • What matters next: Union governance review, public release of details, and the ratification path.

CrewSignal Watch Points

This week’s key development superseded the earlier public focus on unresolved endgame issues. The operative public fact is no longer that the parties were close; it is that they actually reached a tentative agreement.

During the report week, the package was not yet ratified, full public detail had not yet been broadly released, and the next public tests were still ahead.
