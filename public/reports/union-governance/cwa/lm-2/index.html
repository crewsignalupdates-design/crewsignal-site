7. Method and Limits

This page summarizes and flags items visible in CWA’s filed LM-2. Assertions about CWA-AFA net financial benefit, inbound/outbound flows, or fee-for-service equivalence require (1) CWA-AFA’s own LM-2 (or LM-3/LM-4 if applicable), and/or (2) the relevant CWA national schedule-level transaction lines that explicitly name CWA-AFA entities.

Where this filing provides a concrete touchpoint (e.g., the Schedule 11 “AFA-CWA INTL PRESIDENT” entry), we cite it directly. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}

Detailed vendor-level substantiation (legal fees, consulting, political vendors, per-capita counterparties) is recorded in the schedule transaction listings; Crew Signal surfaces those details when they are explicitly itemized in the filing.
