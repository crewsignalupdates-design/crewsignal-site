Purpose and Scope

The purpose of this page is structural clarity. It summarizes how the CWA parent union allocates dues-funded resources across functional categories reported on the LM-2 (representation, political/lobbying, overhead, and administration). Because CWA operates as a union-of-unions, this filing is interpreted as the parent-union side of a broader dues-flow picture.

Cross-union comparisons are deferred to a separate report.

How to Read This Filing

  • Functional categories: LM-2 spending is categorized by function, not by internal beneficiary.
  • Centralized capacity: Parent-union spending often represents shared infrastructure used by multiple affiliates.
  • Attribution limits: The filing does not assign dollars to specific sectors or districts.

What This Page Does Not Claim

  • It does not attribute spending to specific sectors or districts.
  • It does not measure service quality or effectiveness.
  • It does not analyze downstream affiliate budgets.

A complete dues-flow picture requires pairing the parent LM-2 with sector-level filings, which will be analyzed separately after CWA is finalized.
