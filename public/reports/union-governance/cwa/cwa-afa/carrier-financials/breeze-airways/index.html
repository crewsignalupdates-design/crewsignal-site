What the Visible Direct Local Share Means

This metric measures the percentage of visible Breeze-specific CWA-AFA sector funding that went directly to a named Breeze local body rather than to the Breeze MEC.

Formula: direct named Breeze local funding ÷ total visible Breeze-specific CWA-AFA sector funding.

  • FY2025: 0 ÷ 23,697 = 0%.

Treat this as a floor, not a complete service measure. It captures only direct Breeze-specific CWA-AFA sector funding lines that are publicly visible on the reviewed sector filing text.
