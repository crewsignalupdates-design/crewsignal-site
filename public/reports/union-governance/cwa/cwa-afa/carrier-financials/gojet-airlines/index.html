CWA-AFA · Carrier Financials

GoJet Airlines

Tracing money from parent CWA to the CWA-AFA sector to the visible GoJet MEC and local layers.

Quick Read

  • Visible structure: GoJet is clearer than a pure sector-to-MEC carrier because the public record shows visible CWA-AFA sector funding to both the GoJet MEC and a named local body, LEC 26031.
  • Most recent verified standalone filing signal: GoJet MEC LM-3 (FY ending 05/31/2020) reports $68,547 in total receipts and $68,547 in total disbursements.
  • Visible direct local share: 19.6% in FY2025, 33.5% in FY2024, and 27.9% in FY2023.
  • Bottom line: GoJet provides a visible local-dollar throughline, but it is still much more MEC-heavy than carriers where the local share approaches or exceeds half of visible sector funding.

Filing Map

GoJet has to be read in layers. Parent CWA is the upstream dues layer. The CWA-AFA sector LM-2 is the clearest public source for visible GoJet affiliate funding. The older standalone GoJet MEC LM-3 adds a carrier-level spending signal, while the elections record shows an active local council.

Visible CWA-AFA Sector Funding

  • FY2025: AFA MEC GoJet Airlines $54,355; AFA LEC 26031 GoJet Airlines $13,260.
  • FY2024: AFA MEC GoJet Airlines $27,689; AFA LEC 26031 GoJet Airlines $13,931.
  • FY2023: AFA MEC GoJet Airlines $33,362; AFA LEC 26031 GoJet Airlines $12,902.

These are visible CWA-AFA sector allocations to GoJet-identified affiliates. They are not, by themselves, a complete measure of all bargaining, legal, administrative, or parent-supported service delivered to the property.

What the Visible Direct Local Share Means

This metric measures the percentage of visible GoJet-specific CWA-AFA sector funding that went directly to the named GoJet local body rather than to the GoJet MEC.

Formula: direct named GoJet local funding ÷ total visible GoJet-specific CWA-AFA sector funding.

  • FY2025: 13,260 ÷ (54,355 + 13,260) = 19.6%.
  • FY2024: 13,931 ÷ (27,689 + 13,931) = 33.5%.
  • FY2023: 12,902 ÷ (33,362 + 12,902) = 27.9%.

Treat this as a floor, not a complete service measure. It captures only direct GoJet-specific CWA-AFA sector funding lines that are publicly visible on the sector LM-2.

Most Recent Verified MEC Filing Signal

The verified FY ending 05/31/2020 GoJet MEC LM-3 reports $68,547 in total receipts and $68,547 in total disbursements. It also reports $34,893 in disbursements to officers, $4,804 to employees, $24,440 in office and administrative expense, and $4,410 in professional fees.

Because this verified standalone filing is older, the current public money trail for GoJet is still strongest at the sector-allocation layer.

Interpretive Caution

GoJet has a visible local-dollar throughline, but the local share is still relatively modest compared with carriers where a much larger portion of visible sector funding goes straight to local bodies. That makes GoJet a useful mid-range comparator for this project.
