How to Use This Hub

  • Governance Model Overview describes each union’s structure and controlling documents (constitution, bylaws, convention rules, and related primary sources).
  • Democratic Processes outlines election mechanics, voting rights, convention structure, and representation/authority pathways.
  • Committees & Contract Administration explains enforcement architecture: committees, grievance pipelines, and decision-routing.
  • Funding & Financial Transparency (LM-2) shows revenue flows, disbursements, organizing spend, political activity, and parent/subordinate financial signals.
  • Strategic Signals examines institutional behavior revealed through governance posture, bargaining arrangements, alliances, and merger/coordination activity.
