How to Use This Hub

  • Start with “Governance Model Overview” to see each union’s structure and controlling documents (constitution, bylaws, convention rules, and related primary sources).
  • Use “Democratic Processes” for election mechanics, voting rights, convention structure, and representation/authority pathways.
  • Use “Committees & Contract Administration” for enforcement architecture: committees, grievance pipelines, and decision-routing.
  • Use “Funding & Financial Transparency (LM-2)” for revenue flows, disbursements, organizing spend, political activity, and parent/subordinate financial signals.
  • Use “Strategic Signals” for institutional behavior revealed through governance posture, bargaining arrangements, alliances, and merger/coordination activity.
