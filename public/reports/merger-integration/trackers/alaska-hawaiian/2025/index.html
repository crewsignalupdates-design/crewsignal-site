Flight Attendant Integration — 2025 Year-End Review

Scope

This review is limited to the flight attendant employee group and tracks integration signals affecting representation, contract posture, and joint bargaining progress. It does not evaluate fleet/network strategy, financial performance, or non-FA work groups.

2025 Summary

  • Joint contract negotiations began in March 2025 with a Joint Negotiating Committee (JNC) pursuing a Joint Collective Bargaining Agreement (JCBA).
  • Negotiations progressed through multiple sessions during 2025, with periodic section-by-section updates communicated to flight attendants.
  • Key integration risk remains sequencing and pace: how quickly (and in what order) work-rule and economic sections converge into a unified agreement.
