Status Summary

Allegiant / Sun Country produced exact-week public signals on post-close financing and liability management rather than customer-facing or labor-facing integration. On June 23, Allegiant said holders representing 93.68% of the outstanding principal amount of its 2027 senior secured notes had tendered or consented, and on June 24 it said it issued $650.0 million of 7.125% senior secured notes due 2031.

The June 24 release said Allegiant used a portion of the proceeds to purchase and cancel $377.534 million of the 2027 notes, with $25.465 million remaining outstanding and expected redemption in the third quarter of 2026. CrewSignal reads the exact week as continued post-acquisition balance-sheet positioning under the combined company rather than a newly disclosed operating-platform, loyalty, or labor milestone.

Integration Dashboard

  • Allegiant said holders representing 93.68% of the aggregate principal amount of its 7.250% senior secured notes due 2027 delivered consents by the June 23 early tender deadline.
  • Allegiant said it issued $650.0 million in 7.125% senior secured notes due 2031 on June 24.
  • Allegiant said it used a portion of the proceeds to purchase $377.534 million aggregate principal amount of the 2027 notes that were tendered and canceled those notes.
  • Allegiant said $25.465 million aggregate principal amount of the 2027 notes remained outstanding and that it expects to redeem the remainder in the third quarter of 2026.
  • Allegiant’s June 23 and June 24 releases continued to describe the company as serving approximately 22 million annual customers through Allegiant Air and Sun Country Airlines across more than 650 routes and nearly 175 cities.
  • The exact reporting week did not produce a new public single-operating-certificate, loyalty-combination, seniority, or representation milestone.

CrewSignal Watch Points

  • Watch for the first public disclosure on customer-facing integration steps beyond combined-company financing, including distribution, loyalty, or brand sequencing.
  • Watch for the first public timeline on FAA certificate work or operating-platform consolidation.
  • Watch for labor or representation disclosures that clarify how post-close functional integration will affect workgroups at Allegiant and Sun Country.
