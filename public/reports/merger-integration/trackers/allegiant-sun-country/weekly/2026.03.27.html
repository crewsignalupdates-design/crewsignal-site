Week Ending 2026.03.27

No additional public approval, integration step, or labor-sequencing milestone was identified during the week ending 2026.03.27. The most recent material development remains the March 16 announcement that the Hart-Scott-Rodino waiting period ended early for Allegiant’s proposed acquisition of Sun Country.

That development still matters. It means the transaction has cleared its HSR antitrust waiting period, the companies now expect closing in the second or third quarter of 2026, and the key remaining public closing conditions are DOT approval of the interim exemption application plus shareholder approval at both companies.

For flight attendants, this remains a pre-close story rather than a post-close integration story. The regulatory path is materially more advanced than it was at the start of March, but CrewSignal is not identifying a new week-of closing or representation development beyond that cleared HSR stage.
