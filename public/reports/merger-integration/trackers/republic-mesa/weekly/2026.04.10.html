Status Summary

Republic / Mesa produced a clear post-close governance marker during the week ending April 10, 2026. Republic’s proxy materials were made available on or about April 9, and the company’s investor relations SEC filings page listed both a definitive proxy statement and an Annual Report to Security Holders dated April 10, 2026.

The proxy statement also restated the legal mechanics of the November 25, 2025 merger, including that Legacy Republic merged into Mesa, the surviving legal entity was renamed Republic Airways Holdings Inc., and the stock began trading as RJET on the same date. The week therefore read as governance normalization at the combined company rather than as a fresh operating or labor end-state milestone.
