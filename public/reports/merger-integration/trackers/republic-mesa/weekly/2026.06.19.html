Status Summary

Republic / Mesa produced clear governance and executive-transition signals during the week ending June 19, 2026. Republic’s June 17 8-K said Matthew Koscal joined the board effective June 15 as he assumed the CEO role, while David Grizzle returned to non-executive chairman.

The same filing said Chief Financial Officer Joseph Allman and Chief Operating Officer Paul Kinstedt were promoted to Executive Vice President effective June 15, and the concurrent 8-K/A disclosed Koscal’s revised compensation arrangements. The public read for the exact week was leadership normalization after the merger rather than a new labor, certificate, or operating-integration milestone.
