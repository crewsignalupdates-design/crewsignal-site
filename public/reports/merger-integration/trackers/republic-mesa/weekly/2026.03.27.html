Week Ending 2026.03.27

The Republic / Mesa tracker did produce meaningful public signals in this reporting window, even if they were more about sequencing than about a new flight-attendant bulletin. This week reads like a reminder that the transaction is real, but the integration is still actively being staged.

On March 4, Republic told investors that 2026 is a transition year and that integration work with Mesa is expected to continue through 2026 and be substantially complete by the end of 2027. Then, on March 20, the National Mediation Board stated in a Republic mechanics representation case that Republic and Mesa were not yet a single transportation system for representation purposes, even though merger-related oversight and FAA certificate-integration work were underway.

For flight attendants, that is the critical read. The merger is completed at the corporate level, but the operating and representation architecture is still in transition. CrewSignal therefore treats Republic / Mesa as an active integration process rather than a settled end state.
